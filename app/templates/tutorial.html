Shelly Planner

Tutorial

Adding Devices

In the planner, use tabs on the left: Junction Box, Wiring Device, Shelly Devices, and Import. Choose a device card and click + to place it in preview mode, then click on the grid to drop it.

Removing Devices

Click a placed item to select it, then use the trash button in the planner header. If a wire is selected, the same delete action removes only that wire.

Device Types

Shelly devices provide terminal mappings from imported profiles. Wiring devices are custom field devices like switches and receptacles. Junction boxes provide knock-out anchors and can route circuits between devices. Add-ons act as connector modules with mapped ports.

Wiring Devices Together

Click a source anchor such as a junction box knock-out or a device terminal, choose circuit and wire color, then click intermediate waypoints and finally the destination anchor. Repeat for each conductor as needed. KO-to-KO links between junction boxes are drawn as thick gray feeder links.

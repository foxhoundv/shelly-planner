Shelly Planner

Welcome

This web app helps you build and share wiring layouts visually. Place junction boxes, Shelly devices, wiring devices, and add-ons onto the planning grid, then connect terminals with realistic circuit wires.

Use the left panel tabs to add equipment, then drag and position items on the grid. You can manually wire from knock-outs and terminals, or use guided controls to speed up common wiring paths.

  • Plan layouts by dragging items onto the grid.
  • Wire between junction box knock-outs, device terminals, and connector ports.
  • Save your planning progress automatically in your browser session.
  • Export and import complete projects for collaboration.
